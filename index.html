How to Play

Desktop

  • WASDMove
  • MouseLook around
  • ShiftSprint
  • SpaceJump
  • ClickInteract / use tool
  • R-ClickFish at water's edge
  • TabEBackpack & crafting
  • EscPause

Touch

  • ◉Left stick to move
  • ↔Drag right side to look
  • ⤴Jump button
  • ✊Interact button
  • 🎣Fish near water
  • 🎒Open backpack

Tip: stay warm at night by the campfire — exposure drops faster after sundown.
